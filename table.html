Our Reservation

All reservations made online require a emailed confirmation.

If you do not have a email confirmation, sorry you do not have a reservation. Please feel free to walk-in.

No online reservations are available on Saturdays or holidays.Sunday reservations are available from 3pm – 5pm only.

All reservations have a 15 minutes grace period.

All guests must be present in order for your reservation to be seated.

Reservations require a 24 hour notice & must have an email confirmation. Reservations made after 5:00 pm for the same day may not be honored. Feel free to walk in.
