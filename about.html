About Us

The main dish and drink at "MOLIONO" is

"Risotto" and "Masseto"

Risotto is traditionally made in northern Italy, prepared by cooking the rice together with other ingredients in order to transform it into a unique dish. "Risotto" is a dish that tells a story of its territory, its history and its tradition of great italian.

"Every Risotto should be made with seasonal ingredients and accompanied by a good wine of your choice."

Masseto is not only one of the most famous Super Tuscan wines, made entirely from Merlot, . Masseto is made by Tenuta dell’Ornellaia from a blend of small individual plots of Merlot on the Masseto vineyard, a 7-hectare terroir on the coast of Tuscany.

"We offer a high selection of this Italian wines for a complete northern italian experience."
